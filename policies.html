Kaleoscreations Policies

Return Policy

Returns may be made at any point in time as long as product comes back in the original packaging. If a piece is returned without packaging refunds are not offered. Returning a piece does not guaruntee a refund, it is dependent on the condition of the piece. If a piece is still wanted but broken refrence the "repair policy". If a piece is returned in poor condition more then three times then services may be denied.

Refund Policy

Refunds can be made within two weeks with up to a 90% refund, if the piece is entirely untouched with the packaging intact; and up to a 85% refund in the same condition if the piece needed to be shipped. Refunds can be made with up to a 75% refumd if packaging is not intact but the piece is still in good conditon and unworn; and up to a 70% refund it the piece is in the same condition and the piece needed to be shipped. Any case beyond that please refrence the "Return policy" or "repair policy"

Repair Policy

Repairs and remakes can be done at any time no matter if it is one day, one month, or one year after purchase. Repairs will be made free of charge within a month as long as 75% - 80% of the original piece is present and in decent condition. In the case it is 60%-%50 of the original piece it will be repaired at 50% of the original cost of the piece. Anything under 50% of the origional piece is considered a remake and will be done at full price of the original cost of the piece.

Custom piece Policy

Custom pieces and adjustments are more then welcome and encouraged. Though for that to work constant and consistent communication is required through the planning and making of the piece to ensure it comes out as desired. Custom pieces may cost more then average pieces due to extended planning and creation proccess.
